Nag Live at Magnolia House
Magnolia House is a live music venue established in 2012. We host all kinds of music from Hip-Hop to Indie electronic. However we specialize in Punk rock and specifically hardcore Punk. We have become an icon in the Virginia punk community and in the Charlottesville DIY scene. Feel free and explore this website to find more information about shows, artists, genres, and the history related to Magnolia House.
