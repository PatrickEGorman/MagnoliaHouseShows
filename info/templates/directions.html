Parking

  • For Touring Musicians
    There is an alley connecting 10th ST NW and 10 1/2 ST NW running right next to Magnolia House. There are a few parallel spots right next to the house there ideal for musicieans who have to lug gear. Musicians can also park in any of the concert goer parking
  • For Concert Goers
    CB Holt House closes at 5 and there are usually spots in the lot outside of there. 10 1/2 St NW has parellel parking available. There are other spots around! These are just a couple suggestions
